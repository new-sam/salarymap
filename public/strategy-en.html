A · VietnamWorks-style
POSTING FEE
$80/post

B · Wanted-style · PRIMARY
SUCCESS FEE
7% of salary
Risk-free appeal. Familiar to VN SMEs. High unit (~$1.5K/hire). Delayed revenue.
C · Wanted late-stage
SUBSCRIPTION
$200/mo
Predictable MRR. But SaaS resistance in VN — rejected until ROI proven. Later layer.
Test sequence + measurement
D1–14: Free for everyone (Discovery) — drive volume, land first successful hire
D15–28: B as default, offer A to a new cohort in parallel → measure "free + 7% on hire" vs "$80 upfront + free hire" — which books more meetings?
After 5 hires/month: Offer C subscription to paying companies → measure retention vs churn
Key metric: per model (conversion rate × ARPU × CAC payback) — converge on the most efficient model

4Risk Watch Week 1 must-block — BM-critical
Risk
Scenario
Mitigation
PDPL (privacy law)
Exposing full name/resume violates Vietnam PDPL (Decree 13/2023)
Anonymized cards only (ID# · skill · salary · intent). Contact info unlocks only on "Interview Request" click
Disintermediation
Companies see candidates and bypass us via LinkedIn/Facebook → no success fee
Contact info visible only inside platform. All Zalo messages routed through our OA. Direct-contact clause in T&C
First-match quality
One bad match in the first 3 cards = "AI doesn't work" brand stain, hard to recover
Human review all matches before send-out in D1. Automation only after quality stabilizes
Big picture in one line
Anonymized cards + 1-click Zalo CTA to 100 active-hiring companies → 30 onboard for free → service delivery → 7% success fee from first hire.
Build only what supports the BM: Zalo integration · match engine · auto-scheduling · status tracking. Block PDPL · disintermediation · match quality in week 1.
